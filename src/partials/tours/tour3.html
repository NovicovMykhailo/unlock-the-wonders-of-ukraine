Black Sea Jewel

This interesting tour will introduce you to the most beautiful seaside city of Ukraine - Odessa. You will walk along the central Primorsky Boulevard where you can get acquainted with the color of the city, its architecture and unique atmosphere. You will visit the Potemkin Stairs - one of the most famous sights of Odessa, where you can admire the beautiful views of the city and the sea. You will also have a look at the famous Odessa Opera and Ballet Theater. After that you will have a look at the masterpieces of Odessa cuisine in one of the most famous restaurants.
