Lake Synevyr and Shipit waterfall

This very interesting excursion will introduce you to the most beautiful lake of the Carpathians "Synevyr", the impressive and magical waterfall lake Synevirm "Shypit", which is also located in the Ukrainian Carpathians. You will have the opportunity to try Soymivska and Kelechynska mineral springs, drinks and delicious dishes of Carpathian cuisine and, of course, admire the beautiful scenery of the unique Ukrainian Carpathians.

  • Departure from Lviv (07:00) / Stryi (08:00)
  • Pylypets (overview of Shipit waterfall and lunch)
  • Synevyr Pass (photographing and viewing of Carpathian panoramas)
  • "Synevyr" National Park (brown bear rehabilitation center and transition to the lake)
  • Stryi (21:00) / Lviv (return by 22:00)

Included:

  • Transfer
  • Travel insurance
  • Professional guide
  • Travel insurance

Ancient History & Vibrant Present

This interesting excursion will familiarize you with one of the most important shrines of Kiev, St. Sophia Cathedral, which is a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Also you will see one of the main gates of Kiev of the times of Kiev Rus'. Golden Gate. Ambassadors you will be able to bargain at one of the most sterile markets in Kiev - the Bessarabian Market and also taste local delicacies. And closer to the evening you will visit one of the main Orthodox monasteries. Kiev-Pechersk Lavra, described in the 9th century chronicles.

  • Departure from Kiev (08:00)
  • St. Sophia's Cathedral (iconic UNESCO World Heritage site)
  • Golden Gate (historical monument visit)
  • Bessarabsky Market (tasting local delicacies)
  • Kiev Pechersk Lavra (exploration of the significant monastery)
  • Return to Kiev by 20:00

Black Sea Jewel

This interesting tour will introduce you to the most beautiful seaside city of Ukraine - Odessa. You will walk along the central Primorsky Boulevard where you can get acquainted with the color of the city, its architecture and unique atmosphere. You will visit the Potemkin Stairs - one of the most famous sights of Odessa, where you can admire the beautiful views of the city and the sea. You will also have a look at the famous Odessa Opera and Ballet Theater. After that you will have a look at the masterpieces of Odessa cuisine in one of the most famous restaurants.

  • Departure from Odessa (08:00)
  • Primorsky Boulevard (city's scenic walk)
  • Potemkin Steps (historical site and great city view)
  • Odessa Opera and Ballet Theatre (exterior visit)
  • Lunch at a local seafood restaurant
  • Return to Odessa by 20:00
